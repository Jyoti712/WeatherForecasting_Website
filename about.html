About WeatherSphere

WeatherSphere is your go-to platform for real-time, accurate weather forecasts, developed to make weather insights both accessible and actionable. Whether you're planning your day or preparing for severe conditions, we’ve got you covered.

We gather and analyze data from a wide range of reliable sources, including the OpenWeather API, to bring you hyperlocal, detailed forecasts. Our mission is to provide clear, accurate, and beautiful weather updates that help people stay informed and safe.

Launched in 2023, WeatherSphere continues to evolve with a user-first approach, combining modern design with trusted meteorological science. We’re committed to constant improvement and value your feedback.
