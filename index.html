About WeatherSphere

WeatherSphere is a cutting-edge weather application that provides accurate, real-time weather forecasts for locations worldwide. Our platform combines data from multiple sources, including the OpenWeather API, to deliver the most reliable weather information.

Founded in 2023, our mission is to make weather information accessible and understandable for everyone, from casual users to professional meteorologists.
